Naming the Actual Threshold

Every transition asks a version of the same question: what exactly are you crossing from, and into? This note offers a simple language exercise you can do after the video to name your own threshold more honestly.

For couples & families

When One Person is Ready First

In many transitions, one person has language long before the others. This short reflection introduces ways to talk about “different speeds” of change without blame, and to build a shared pace over time.

Organizational change

Announcements vs. Accompaniment

Organizations are good at announcing change and less practiced at accompanying people through it. This note points to a few small, human gestures you can add around any big announcement to help it land.
